Welcome to my New York Times Best Sellers preview application! I have always found it difficult to choose books from the New York Times Best Sellers lists due to a lack of context. I set out to build an app that allows you to preview each book and see if it is worth a read. After pressing "Enter" below, please select a genre that interests you. This will pull up the most recently published best sellers list. From here you can select a book cover in order to view a summary and preview the book. Enjoy!
